About Me

Data Analytics • BI • SQL • Power BI

Welcome to my portfolio — a place built out of equal parts curiosity, frustration, and a genuine love for clarity. My mantra is simple: what gets measured gets improved.

Working in operations showed me just how chaotic real-world data can be, and how powerful it is when you cut through the noise and make things make sense. This portfolio is my way of showing what I’ve learned: how to clean that data, model it, visualize it, and most importantly, extract insights that actually matter.

At Rocket Mortgage, I focused on surfacing operational issues, improving visibility into performance trends, operational cost, and helping teams understand and resolve bottlenecks. To showcase these skills publicly, I engineered a fully synthetic mortgage company and built a complete analytics environment around it: Python‑generated data, a full ETL pipeline, a relational SQL database, and multiple Power BI dashboards.

My goal is always the same: I don’t want to just build dashboards. I want to build analytics that cut through the noise and are actually used in decision making — tools that bring clarity, drive action, and make people’s jobs easier.
